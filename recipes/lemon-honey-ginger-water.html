Ingredients

  • 1 to 2 inches fresh ginger
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 2 teaspoons raw honey

Steps

  1. Grate the ginger into 2 cups of water.
  2. Allow the water to steep for at least 15 minutes and ideally longer.
  3. You can even leave it steeping in the fridge overnight if you wish.
  4. Strain the ginger out of the water when you’re ready to drink it, add the lemon juice and raw honey.
